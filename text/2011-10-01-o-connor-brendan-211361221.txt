MEDIA RELEASE



Saturday, 1 October 2011



Border Protection Command intercepts vessel



HMAS Albany, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, intercepted  a suspected irregular entry vessel north of Christmas Island today.

Initial indications suggest that there are 70 passengers and two crew on board.

The vessel was initially detected by a RAAF P3 aircraft, operating under the control of  Border Protection Command.

The passengers will be transferred to Christmas Island, where they will undergo initial  checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
