GIFTS TO OVERSEAS AID FUNDS

Gifts to the following overseas aid funds established to provide relief for  persons in developing countries will be tax deductible from 3 February  1992:-

Daughters of our Lady of the Sacred Heart Overseas Aid Fund  Divine Word Missionaries Overseas Aid Fund

To be deductible gifts must be $2 or more and be made on or after  3 February 1992.
