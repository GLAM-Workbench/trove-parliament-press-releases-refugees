Biographies

Ms Stephanie Brakespeare  Ms Brakespeare has over 14 years of experience as a full-time and sessional member of the  former Social Security Appeals Tribunal and the Administrative Appeals Tribunal. She has  previously been the Deputy Chairperson of the Prisoners Review Board in Western Australia.

Ms Brakespeare holds a Bachelor of Arts and a Practitioner’s certificate in mediation.

Mrs Danica Buljan  Mrs Buljan is a dispute resolution specialist with the Telecommunications Industry  Ombudsman. She has 15 years of experience as a member of the former Migration Review  Tribunal and Refugee Review Tribunal. Ms Buljan has previously been the Deputy Registrar  of the Refugee Review Tribunal.

Mrs Buljan holds a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws.

Ms Anna Burke  Ms Burke is currently a Director of Tolpuddle Pty Ltd. Previously she was a Member of the  House of Representatives. During her time in Federal Parliament, Ms Burke was the Speaker  of the House of Representatives. Ms Burke currently sits on a number of boards.

Ms Burke holds a Bachelor of Arts and Master of Commerce.

Ms Michelle East  Ms East is currently an arbitrator for Workcover Western Australia. She has previous  experience as a Conference Registrar for the Administrative Appeals Tribunal and 15 years  of experience as a lawyer for the Australian Government Solicitor.

Ms East holds a Bachelor of Jurisprudence and a Bachelor of Laws. She was admitted to the  Supreme Court of Western Australia and the High Court of Australia in 1992.

Mrs Lana Gallagher  Mrs Gallagher is a Senior Associate with Moray & Agnew Lawyers. She has over 10 years of  legal experience with a number of firms in Australia and London.

Mrs Gallagher holds a Bachelor of Arts and a Bachelor of Laws. She was admitted to the  Supreme Court of New South Wales and the Supreme Court of Western Australia in 2005.

Mr Milton Griffin QC  Mr Griffin QC is a former Judge of the District Court of Queensland. He has over 30 years of  experience as a barrister and as Crown Prosecutor.

Mr Griffin QC holds a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws. He was admitted as a  Barrister of the Supreme Court of Queensland in 1975.



Contact:   Office of Attorney-General: T: +61 2 6277 7300  E: agmedia@ag.gov.au



Dr Colin Huntly  Dr Huntly is a Clerk Assistant with the Legislative Council of Western Australia. He was  previously the Principal Policy Advisor to the Hon Dr Liz Constable MLA, Minister for  Education in Western Australia. Dr Huntly has also held positions with the Legislative  Council of Tasmania and the Western Australian Legislative Council.

Dr Huntly holds a Bachelor of Business, Bachelor of Laws and a Doctor of Philosophy in  Law.

Mr Michael Judd  Mr Judd is currently a Principal Legal Officer with the Australian Criminal Intelligence  Commission. He has 15 years of experience as a lawyer with a number of state and  Commonwealth agencies. He also has extensive experience as a police officer.

Mr Judd holds a Bachelor of Laws. He was admitted to the Supreme Court of Western  Australia in 1999 and the High Court of Australia in 2012.

Ms Linda Kirk  Ms Kirk is currently the Deputy Director and Sub-Dean for the Migration Law Program at  the Australian National University College of Law. She has five years of previous experience  as a senior member of the former Migration Review Tribunal and Refugee Review Tribunal.  She has previously been a Senator for South Australia.

Ms Kirk holds a Master of Laws and Bachelor of Laws. She was admitted to the Supreme  Court of South Australia in 1991 and the High Court of Australia in 1992.

Mr Russell Matheson  Mr Matheson was a Police Sergeant in the New South Wales Police Force for 15 years and a  Councillor for the Campbelltown City Council for 20 years, where he spent time as the  Mayor. He is a former member of the House of Representatives.

Dr Graham Maynard  Dr Maynard has 10 years of experience as a part-time medical member of the Administrative  Appeals Tribunal. He has previously been a Chief Medical Advisor for the Therapeutic  Goods Administration. He has also been the Director of Australian Government Health  Services and the Director of Medical Services in the Australian Army.

Dr Maynard holds a Master of Occupational Medicine and a Diploma of Industrial Health  and Diploma of Tropical Medicine and Hygiene.

Mr Alan McMurran  Mr McMurran is currently the Principal of AM Legal Solutions. He is also a part-time  member of the New South Wales Civil and Administrative Tribunal. He has over 30 years of  experience as a solicitor.



Contact:   Office of Attorney-General: T: +61 2 6277 7300  E: agmedia@ag.gov.au



Mr McMurran holds a Bachelor of Laws and a Bachelor of Arts. He was admitted to the  Supreme Court of New South Wales.

Ms Alison Murphy  Ms Murphy is a current member of the Victorian Civil and Administrative Tribunal. She is  also a member of the Nauru Refugee Status Review Tribunal. She has previously held  positions with the former Social Security Appeals Tribunal and the former Migration Review  Tribunal and Refugee Review Tribunal.

Ms Murphy holds a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws. She was admitted to the  Supreme Court of New South Wales in 1996, the Supreme Court of Western Australia in  1997, the Supreme Court of Queensland in 2001 and the Supreme Court of Victoria in 2003.

Mr Andrew Nikolic AM CSC  Mr Nikolic commenced his military career with the Australian Army in 1979. He served as  Deputy Commander and Chief of Staff of Australian Forces Middle East in Afghanistan from  2001-2002; and as Australian National Commander, Southern Iraq in 2005. Mr Nikolic held a  number of senior public official positions, including as First Assistant Secretary with the  Department of Defence from 2006-2013. He is a former member of the House of  Representatives.

Mr Nikolic holds a Master of Strategic Studies, Master of Arts and a Master of Management  Studies.

Mr Chris Packer  Mr Packer is currently a Review Panel Member for the Papua New Guinea Immigration and  Citizenship Service Authority. He has 10 years of experience as a member of the former  Migration Review Tribunal and Refugee Review Tribunal. He was also an Independent  Reviewer for the Independent Protection Assessment Office.

Mr Packer holds a Bachelor of Laws. He has been admitted to the Supreme Court of  South Australia and the High Court of Australia.

Ms Kim Parker  Ms Parker is an Assistant Special Counsel at the Australian Competition and Consumer  Commission. She also has over 13 years of experience as a solicitor and principal with a  number of law firms.

Ms Parker holds a Bachelor of Science and Bachelor of Laws. She was admitted to the  Supreme Court of Victoria in 1993 and the Supreme Court of New South Wales in 2001.
