Border Protection Command boards vessel

HMAS Pirie, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, intercepted a  suspected irregular entry vessel north west of Ashmore Islands yesterday afternoon.

Initial indications suggest there are 48 passengers and four crew on board.

The vessel was initially detected by a RAAF P-3 aircraft, operating under the control of Border  Protection Command.

The group will be transferred to Christmas Island where they will undergo security, identity  and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
