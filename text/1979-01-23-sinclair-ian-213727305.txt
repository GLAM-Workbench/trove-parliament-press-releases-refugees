The Acting Minister for Foreign Affairs, Mr Ian Sinclair,

said today the Government has reviewed its relations with

Vietnam in the light of Vietnam's military involvement in

Kampuchea and its attitude to the refugee question.

The Government decided to suspend its ongoing aid programme

to Vietnam and also to suspend all cultural exchanges.

The exception to this decision is that students presently

'  undertaking courses in Australia will be allowed to finish

these courses.
