MEDIA RELEASE

Monday, 21 March 2011



Border Protection Command intercepts vessel

HMAS Maitland, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, intercepted a  suspected irregular entry vessel east of Ashmore Islands this morning.

Initial indications suggest there are 57 passengers and four crew on board.

The vessel was initially detected by a RAAF P3 surveillance aircraft, operating under the control of  Border Protection Command.

The group will be transferred to immigration detention on the mainland where they will undergo  security, identity and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
