Border Protection Command intercepts vessel

Customs and Border Protection officers boarded a suspected irregular entry vessel north west  of Christmas Island this afternoon.

Initial indications suggest there are 49 passengers and two crew on board the vessel.

The group will be transferred to Christmas Island where they will undergo security, identity  and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
