Appointments to the Migration Review Tribunal MPS 124/2000

Four new full-time members and nine new part-time members have been appointed to the Migration Review Tribunal (MRT), the Minister for Immigration and Multicultural Affairs, Philip Ruddock, announced today.

The MRT provides a single tier of review of general migration decisions made by the Department of Immigration and Multicultural Affairs.

"These new full-time and part-time members have had distinguished professional careers in the Australian community in a variety of fields and will bring a wealth of experience to the Tribunal."

The new appointees to the MRT are as follows:

Full-time

George Haddad ACT Amanda MacDonald NSW James Mahoney VIC William Wilde ACT

Part-time

Alexander Bates QLD William Bowen WA Janis Butt ACT

Andrew Carson VIC John Cipolla NSW Jonathon Duignan NSW Ann Graham VIC Alison Mercer VIC Linda Pearson NSW

The appointments will cease on 30 June 2001 when the MRT is expected to be amalgamated into new Administrative Review Tribunal (ART), which is due to commence on 1 July 2001.
