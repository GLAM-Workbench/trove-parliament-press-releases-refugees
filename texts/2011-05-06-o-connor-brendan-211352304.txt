Border Protection Command boards vessel

HMAS Launceston, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, boarded a  suspected irregular entry vessel north of Broome yesterday afternoon.

Initial indications suggest there are 63 people on board.

The group will be transferred to Christmas Island where they will undergo security, identity  and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
