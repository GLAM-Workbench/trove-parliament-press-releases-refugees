Gerry Hand, MPParliament House, Canberra, A C T 2600  Telephone: (06) 2 7 7  7860MPS 68/92REMOVAL OF ILLEGAL ENTRANTS FROM AUSTRALIAThe Minister for Immigration, Local Government and Ethnic Affairs, Mr Gerry Hand, reaffirmed today the Government's determination to remove people who- had no legal basis to enter or remain in Australia.His comment follows the removal this morning of 113 boat people from China who arrived by boat at Christmas Island on Friday, October 30."The people arrived in Australia without authority, expecting they would be allowed to enter Australia and take up jobs. ."While it is apparent they are victims of a scheme to travel to Australia, I endorse the judgement that illegal arrivals who have no legal basis to enter or remain in Australia will not be permitted to do so."In the case of these people, there are assurances from the Chinese Government that they will not be punished as a result of their illegal departure from China."I warn all those who might contemplate travel to Australia in similar circumstances that where there is no legal basis for entering Australia they will be dealt with firmly and quickly," Mr Hand said.CANBERRA 7 November 1992MEDIA INQUIRIES: Lynne AdamsMinister's Office (06) 277 7860 bh (06) 247 7518 ahCOMMONWEALTHPARLIAMENTARY LIBRARY MiCAH
