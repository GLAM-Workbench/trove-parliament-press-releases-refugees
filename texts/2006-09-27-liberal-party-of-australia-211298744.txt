







PRIME MINISTER



MINISTERIAL ARRANGEMENTS

The Administrator Her Excellency Professor Marie Bashir AC CVO has  approved my announcing the following change to the Ministry.

The Hon De-Anne Kelly MP will continue to serve as Parliamentary Secretary  to the Hon Mark Vaile MP when he becomes Minister for Transport and  Regional Services.  Her role as Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for

Transport and Regional Services will be to generally assist Mr Vaile in that  portfolio.

The Minister for Local Government, Territories and Roads, the Hon Jim Lloyd  MP will retain his current responsibilities, including for roads and regional  partnerships.
