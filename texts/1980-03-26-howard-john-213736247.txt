STATEMENT BY. THE TREASURER,. THE. HON J. W. HOWARD, MP

OVERSEAS VISIT

I will be travelling overseas from 20 April to

3 May 1980 to attend meetings of the IMF and

World Bank CtBRD) and the Asian Development Bank.

The IMF/IBRD Development Committee and the IMF

Interim Committee will be meeting in Hamburg

on 24 and 25 April respectively.

The Annual Meeting of the Asian Development Bank,

will be held in Manila from. 30 April to 2 May 1980.

The period between the Hamburg and Manile meetings

will provide ah opportunity to visit Zurich where

I will have talks with leading Swiss bankers.

26 March 1980

Canberra
