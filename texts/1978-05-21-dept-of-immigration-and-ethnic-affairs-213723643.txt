VIETNAMESE VESSEL SIGHTED

A Vietnamese vessel carrying 30 people is expected to

reach Darwin later tonight.

It was sighted by the lighthouse keeper at Cape Don

earlier today and the Navy patrol boat HMAS Aware was sent

to investigate.

The people on board are believed to comprise 12 men,

7 women and 11 children.

This will be the 43rd small boat arrival in Australia,

The usual health, quarantine and immigration procedures

will begin as soon as the vessel reaches Darwin.
