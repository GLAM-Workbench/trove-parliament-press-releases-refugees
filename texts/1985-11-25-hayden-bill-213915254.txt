THAI/CAMBODIA BORDER REGION -  AUSTRALIAN  HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE

The Minister for Foreign Affairs, Mr Bill Hayden,

MP, today announced that Australia would provide $775,000

in food aid to assist displaced Cambodians in the

Thai/Cambodia border region.

Mr Hayden said that the Australian contribution

would be provided to the United Nations Border Relief

Organisation (UNBRO)  for the feeding of Cambodian civilians,

other than Khmer Rouge, in UNBRO camps.

The contribution forms part of the Australian

Government's humanitarian response to refugee situations

throughout the world and is provided from aid funds

administered by the Australian Development Assistance Bureau.
