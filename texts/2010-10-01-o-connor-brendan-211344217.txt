Border Protection Command intercepts vessel

HMAS Maitland, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, intercepted a  suspected irregular entry vessel north of Ashmore Islands early this morning.

Initial indications suggest there are 23 passengers and four crew on board the vessel.

The group will be transferred to Christmas Island where they will undergo security, identity  and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
