I.C.E.M. DIRECTOR IN CANBERRA

The Director of the Intergovernmental Committee on European

Migration, Mr. John F. Thomas, arrived in Canberra today.

He will be in Canberra for four days and will have talks

with the Minister for Immigration, Mr. Phillip Lynch., and senior

ufficers of Government departments.

The talks will cover a wide range of topics, including the

future activities of I.C.E.M., the organisation's present operations

and the extension of language and vocational training for migrants.

I.C.E.M., whose head effice is in Geneva, is supported by

31 member governments. Australia is a foundation member of the

organisation.
