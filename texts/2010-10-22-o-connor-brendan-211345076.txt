Border Protection Command intercepts vessel

HMAS Ararat, operating under the control of Border Protection Command, intercepted a  suspected irregular entry vessel north-west of Ashmore Islands yesterday evening.

Initial indications suggest there are 79 passengers and three crew on board.

The group will be transferred to Christmas Island where they will undergo security, identity  and health checks and their reasons for travel will be established.
